How the HC is calculated — step by step
1
Volume split: Before break = (DNZ2 + ONW3) × 45% + SD_A × 60% · After break = (DNZ2 + ONW3) × 55% + SD_A × 40%
2
BRT (net work time): Phase 1: gross − 7 min SOS · Phase 2: gross − 25 min (EOS + short breaks)
3
Oversize penalty: Induction rate × (normal% + OV%/1.5) · Stower PPH × (normal% + OV%/2) — large packages slow both processes
4
Required speed: Phase volume ÷ BRT = pkgs/hr needed from the belt
5
Induction machines: ⌈Speed ÷ effective induction rate⌉ · Tables: ASL1(3), ASL2(2), ASL3(2), ASML1(3), ASML2(3), ASML3(3), 7th = Flex Conveyor(3 SAs + 1 Cart Runner) · Cart runner = 1/machine
6
Sort SAs per aisle: ⌈(Phase vol × 50%) ÷ effective PPH ÷ BRT⌉ × 2 aisles (A + D)
7
Pickers ONW3: 1 per 2,500 packages · split 45/55 · additional HC
8
Pickers SD_A: 4 aisles · 2h · 50 pkg/hr/person · additional HC — sort floor needs full team for continuous DNZ2+ONW3 volume during pick window
9
ADTA Straighteners: 2 if speed < 10k · 4 if speed ≥ 10k (2/position × 2 positions)
9b
NC Stowers: 1 base · +1 after break if oversize volume ≥ 4,500 pkgs (total vol × OV%)
10
Fixed roles: YM 2 + Quality 3 + PS 4 + Jackpots 2 + NC 1 + PS Bin 1 + C Pusher 1 + Water Spider 3 + ATLAS 1 + Cart Runners + ADTA
11
Total HC: Induction + Sort + ONW3 Pickers + Fixed — no buffer multiplier applied
12
NS + MCO: NS = before break HC · MCO = after break − before break (extra heads) · Total = NS + MCO
13
Available HC: Planned HC × (1 − Absence Rate %) · Delta = Available − Needed → VET if negative, VTO if positive

Data sources: Stower PPH from processInspector BOX-TYPE CSV (weekly) or default 350 · ONW3 pickers: 1 per 2,500 pkgs · SD_A pickers: 4 aisles / 2h / 50 pkg/hr (additional HC) · Volume from PerfectMile STVM · SD_A forecast from QuickSight · Planned HC from AT+ shift plan · Absence rate from Caravan (deducted from planned HC) · Oversize % from OV OFD QuickSight (degrades induction ×1.5 and stow ×2) · Cross-midnight shifts handled automatically (e.g. 23:45 start)
